Web Development

During our third year in college, we created a personal portfolio website using HTML and CSS to showcase our skills, projects, and achievements in web development. The project focused on designing a responsive and visually appealing portfolio that highlights personal information, educational background, and completed systems or projects. Through this activity, we learned the importance of user interface design, webpage structure, and styling techniques in creating professional-looking websites. The project also enhanced our creativity and technical skills in front-end development, particularly in applying layouts, colors, animations, and responsive designs using CSS. By developing our own portfolio, we gained practical experience in building websites that can be used for personal branding and future career opportunities in the field of information technology.
