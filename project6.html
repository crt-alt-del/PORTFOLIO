Quantitative Research

During my 2nd year as a BSIS student, we conducted a research study entitled “Investigating the Impact of ICT Web Development Learning on Student Perception in a Third Year BSIS Program among Colleges in Panabo City.” The study focused on understanding how learning web development through ICT influenced students’ skills, confidence, and perception toward Information Systems projects. Through surveys and data analysis, we discovered that web development learning helped students improve their coding knowledge, problem-solving abilities, and technological skills. The research also highlighted the challenges students face, such as adapting to complex programming tasks and rapidly changing technologies. This experience enhanced my knowledge in research, data gathering, teamwork, and web development concepts while strengthening my interest in the field of Information Systems.
